Basic bicycle inspection

(1) Check your bicycle tire pressure to see if it is normal. Note that this is not a simple matter of squeezing your tire. Each tire has a marked air pressure value. Click here to see the detailed operation.

(2) Adjust your seat height. Too high or too low a seat height will affect your riding experience and is more likely to cause danger. Here is a simple trick to adjust your seat height. Click here.

(3) Make sure your brakes work well. If you need to squeeze the brakes very hard to stop, then you need to check them. Here is a 5-minute tutorial to help you adjust them. Click here.

(4) Make sure your bicycle must be equipped with a bell. If you ride at night, lights and reflectors must also be installed. If you don't have them, you will be fined. This is also for your own safety.
