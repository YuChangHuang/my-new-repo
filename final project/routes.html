Cycling Routes

Finding bike routes using apps and advises against mobile phone use while cycling in the Netherlands, recommending safer alternatives.

How to find the nearest bicycle route to a place?

There are many ways. The most commonly used one is to use the bicycle mode in Google Maps. In this way, the map will plan the route you need to take. One thing to note is that you cannot use your mobile phone to look at the map while riding a bicycle, because it is illegal to ride a bicycle with a mobile phone in the Netherlands. So you can buy a meter with a navigation function, which is safer and more convenient, or turn on your phone's speaker to listen to the navigation.

If you want to go cycling for fun, it is very easy to find a scenic cycling route. There are many guides on the Internet. There are also many sports apps on mobile phones that support route selection, such as Strava, iGPSPORT, etc., which have detailed route data.
